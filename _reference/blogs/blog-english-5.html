How DXN's Income Model Works

DXN uses a single-level marketing structure — meaning every distributor joins under a sponsor and builds their own network downward. There are no complex binary legs, no forced matrices, and no confusing placement rules. The income you earn is directly tied to two things: the products you and your team sell, and the size and activity of your distributor network.

Everything in DXN's compensation plan revolves around one unit: the Point Value (PV). Every product you purchase carries a PV, and your monthly PV — combined with the PV of your entire downline — determines your income level. The higher your total group PV, the higher your commission percentage.

The Four Ways You Earn with DXN

🛒

Retail Profit

Buy products at distributor price, sell at retail price. The difference is your immediate profit — typically 25% on every sale. No monthly targets required to earn this.

📊

Group Bonus

A percentage commission on your total group PV every month. This is the core recurring income — it grows as your team grows and stays active.

⭐

Star Group Bonus

Once you qualify as a Star Agent or above, you earn additional bonuses on the groups led by your direct Star-qualified distributors below you.

🌍

International Profit Sharing

Crown Ambassadors and above participate in DXN's global profit-sharing pool — a percentage of total worldwide sales distributed among the top rank holders.

Understanding Point Value (PV)

Every time you or someone in your downline purchases a DXN product, that purchase generates a Point Value. Your monthly group PV is the sum of all purchases made by you and every distributor in your entire network that month. This total group PV is what determines your commission percentage for that month.

What Does It Actually Take?

The honest answer is: consistent daily effort over months, not weeks. DXN is not a get-rich-quick scheme and it does not work if you sign up and wait for things to happen. What it does offer is a business model with very low startup cost, no inventory risk, and income potential that scales with the size and activity of your team.

Most successful DXN distributors in the UAE follow a simple pattern: they use the products personally, share their genuine experience on social media and WhatsApp, introduce two to five people per month to the business, and support those people consistently through weekly Zoom training. Done consistently over six to twelve months, this builds real, compounding group volume.

The distributors who earn significant income are not the ones who recruited the most people in month one — they are the ones who kept showing up, kept training their team, and kept growing steadily over time.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to buy a large stock of products to start?

No. DXN does not require you to purchase bulk inventory upfront. Most distributors start by ordering products for personal use, building genuine experience, and then sharing that experience with others. You order what you need, when you need it.

How do I get paid — cash, bank transfer, or points?

DXN pays commissions directly to your registered bank account each month, once your earnings reach the minimum payout threshold. The exact payment process and currency depend on your country of registration.

Can I earn income without recruiting anyone?

Yes — through retail profit. If you buy products at distributor price and sell them to customers at retail price, you keep the margin. However, the group bonus and passive income components only grow when you build a team.

Is there a monthly sales quota I have to meet?

DXN does not impose forced monthly purchase quotas. Your commission is based on actual group PV that month. If your group activity is lower in a given month, your commission for that month will be lower — but you do not lose your rank or downline.
