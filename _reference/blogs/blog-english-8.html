What Are RG and GL?

If you have ever explored DXN products, you have almost certainly come across RG and GL. They are DXN's two flagship Ganoderma capsule supplements — sold individually and most commonly as a pair. Between them, they represent the most complete way to consume the full spectrum of Ganoderma Lucidum's active compounds in a concentrated, convenient form.

The key distinction between them comes down to which part of the Ganoderma mushroom they are made from. RG comes from the mature fruiting body — the visible cap of the mushroom. GL comes from the mycelium — the root-like structure that grows beneath the surface before the mushroom fully develops. Both parts of the mushroom contain valuable compounds, but they are different compounds. This is why DXN recommends taking them together.

Ganocelium

  • Higher concentration of organic germanium

Because the fruiting body and the mycelium contain different ratios of active compounds, taking both together gives you a broader, more complete nutritional profile than either one alone. This is the core reason DXN developed and markets them as a pair — and why the majority of long-term DXN members take both daily.

What Is Actually Inside Each Capsule?

Both RG and GL contain the same categories of active compounds found in Ganoderma, but in different concentrations depending on the growth stage and part of the mushroom used. Here is a simplified breakdown of what makes these capsules valuable:

Key Active Compounds in RG & GL

  • Polysaccharides Present in both RG and GL. Beta-glucans in this group are among the most researched compounds for immune system support in medicinal mushroom science.
  • Triterpenoids More concentrated in RG (fruiting body). The bitter compounds that give Ganoderma its characteristic taste. Studied for liver support and anti-inflammatory properties.
  • Organic Germanium More concentrated in GL (mycelium). A trace element found in higher levels in Ganoderma than most other natural sources. Subject of ongoing research interest.
  • Adenosine Present in both. A naturally occurring compound associated with relaxation, circulation support, and improved sleep quality.
  • Proteins & Amino Acids GL in particular contains a wider range of amino acids due to the active metabolic state of the mycelium at 18 days of growth.
